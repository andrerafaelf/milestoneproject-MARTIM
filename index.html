The beggining of what may be portugal's biggest star

Born in 2010, Martim always dreamt about cars and racing at high speeds. In 2017 he picked up his first go-kart and instantly fell in love.


Testimonials

In his first year, Martim ended the 2017 season in 2nd place overall, registering important victories and showing some talent amongst the most experienced. In his second year, Martim won all the races throughout the 2018 season and became campion of his category. His above-average skills were well evidenced in the honorable 3rd place won in 2019, the year in which he competed in a new category, consequently, he was the youngest in the race.

In 2020, Martim and his team defined a project that would host regional and national events, where Martim absolutely smashed his opponents and won all races to become regional and national champion.

—Will Buxton

Nobody else is good enough to come and play with him.

Ever since Martim first stepped into a go-kart he's been absolutely phenomenal. Flying around every single corner of every single track he's competed on. He's a very promising driver who's still very very young. As we all know, pursuing a driver career can be expensive so we'll see how he and his team get on.

—Martin Brundle
